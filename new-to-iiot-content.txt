Section  1: What  Is  IIoT?  (Explained  in  30 
Seconds )
The  IndustrialInternet  of  Things  (IIoT) connects  your  factory ’s  machines , people , 
and  processes  so  they  can  share  data  in  realtime .
Think  of  it  like  giving  your  machines :
• Eyes  (to  see  what ’s  happening ) 
• Ears  (to  listen  to  issues ) 
• Memory  (to  store  performance  data ) 
• A  brain  (to  provide  insights ) 
It  helps  your  factory  make  better  decisions  — automatically .
