Overview

What is the Control Tower Dashboard?

The Petrus Control Tower is the single unified intelligence hub that aggregates live data from PMS, CMS, QMS, EMS, Traceability, and Assembly Tracking — presenting the entire factory's health on one configurable dashboard accessible from any screen, anywhere.

Whether you manage one factory floor or ten plants across different cities, the Control Tower provides a consistent, role-appropriate view of what's happening right now — no phone calls, no WhatsApp updates, no morning reports needed.

Each role gets a tailored control tower view — Plant Head sees OEE and downtime; CXO sees multi-plant performance; Maintenance sees machine health; Quality Head sees rejection trends — all live, all from one platform.

Control Tower aggregates:

  • ✓ Real-time OEE across all lines and plants
  • ✓ Production vs. plan — shift and daily
  • ✓ Machine health & maintenance alerts
  • ✓ Quality rejection trends and FPY
  • ✓ Energy consumption and peak demand
  • ✓ Assembly WIP and Andon status
  • ✓ Exception escalation and response times
  • ✓ Multi-plant comparison dashboards

Core Features

Plant-Wide Live Dashboard

Single screen with live OEE, production count, downtime status, and quality rate for every line on the floor. Wall-mounted TV displays or desktop — configurable layout.

Multi-Plant Comparison

Compare OEE, throughput, rejection rates, and energy across plants in real time. Spot which plant is underperforming and drill into root causes immediately.

Exception & Alert Management

All critical alerts from all modules flow into one exception feed. Prioritised by severity. Response time SLAs tracked. Auto-escalation if unresolved.

Role-Based Views

Each role sees a curated view: Plant Head sees OEE; Maintenance sees machine health; CXO sees multi-plant financials; Quality Head sees reject trends. All live.

Automated KPI Reports

Daily, weekly, and monthly performance reports are generated and emailed automatically. No analyst needed to produce the Monday morning report.

Custom KPI Builder

Build custom KPIs from raw data using the formula builder. Visualise as gauges, trend charts, or heatmaps. Embed in any view in minutes.

How the Control Tower Works

1
Data Aggregation — All modules (PMS, CMS, QMS, EMS, AT) feed data into the unified Petrus data lake in real time via the Edge Gateway.
2
KPI Computation — Pre-built and custom KPIs are calculated on live data streams — OEE, MTBF, FPY, energy per unit, response time — every 15 seconds.
3
Role-Specific Display — Each user role is assigned a dashboard template. Login determines which KPIs, drilldowns, and alert feeds are shown.
4
Exception Feed — Every alert from every module surfaces in the Control Tower timeline. Owners are assigned. Resolution is tracked and closed digitally.
5
Automated Reports — Daily and weekly reports are auto-generated and distributed to configured recipients — no analyst dependency, no delay.
