Overview

What is the Quality Management System?

The Petrus QMS replaces physical inspection sheets, paper NCRs, and Excel-based defect logs with a fully digital, real-time quality control platform that operates at every production stage.

Inspectors use tablets or touch panels to log inspection results directly at the machine. Every defect is classified, photographed, and linked to the machine, operator, batch, and shift — creating an immutable audit trail.

Statistical Process Control (SPC) charts monitor critical dimensions against control limits in real time. When a process drifts, the system alerts quality engineers immediately — before a full batch goes out of spec.

QMS covers:

  • ✓ Incoming material inspection
  • ✓ In-process quality checks (WIP gates)
  • ✓ Final inspection & release
  • ✓ SPC for critical parameters
  • ✓ Non-Conformance Reports (NCR)
  • ✓ CAPA management & closure
  • ✓ Gauge calibration tracking
  • ✓ Customer complaint management

Core Features

Digital Inspection Gates

Mandatory digital checkpoints at receiving, in-process, and final stages. Parts cannot advance without quality sign-off. Fully configurable pass/fail criteria.

Defect Pareto Analysis

Auto-ranked Pareto charts showing top defect types by frequency, cost, machine, and operator. Instantly identify where to focus improvement efforts.

Real-Time SPC Charts

X-bar, R-chart, Cp, Cpk displayed live for key dimensions. Automatic out-of-control signals and actionable alerts when process capability degrades.

NCR & CAPA Management

Log Non-Conformances digitally with photos and measurements. Assign CAPAs with owners and deadlines. Track closure rates and recurrence reduction.

Audit-Ready Digital Records

Every inspection result, sign-off, and NCR is timestamped and stored. Export audit packs in minutes instead of spending days searching paper records.

Gauge Calibration Tracking

Manage calibration schedules for all measuring instruments. Auto-alerts before due dates. Calibration status visible on every inspection form.
