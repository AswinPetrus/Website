Overview

What is the Production Monitoring System?

The Petrus PMS automatically connects to your machines — PLCs, CNCs, conveyors, manual stations — via hardware sensors or protocol integrations, and streams every pulse, count, and stop event into a live dashboard.

No operator data entry. No Excel reports. No blind spots. Just real-time factory intelligence visible from any device — whether you're on the floor or in a board meeting.

It replaces daily production reports, whiteboard tracking, and gut-feel decisions with live machine data classified automatically into Availability, Performance, and Quality — the three pillars of OEE.

PMS connects to:

  • ✓ CNC machines (Fanuc, Siemens, Allen Bradley)
  • ✓ PLCs (Siemens S7, Allen Bradley, Schneider)
  • ✓ Conveyors and packaging lines
  • ✓ Manual stations (operator touch panels)
  • ✓ Legacy machines (pulse/counter sensors)
  • ✓ Injection moulding machines
  • ✓ Spinning, weaving and textile machines
  • ✓ Forging presses and stamping machines

Core Features

Live OEE Dashboard

Real-time OEE per machine, line, and plant. Drill down into Availability, Performance, and Quality components instantly.

Shift & Daily Reports

Auto-generated shift summary — planned vs. actual output, top downtime codes, per-machine performance ranking.

Downtime Categorisation

Every stoppage is auto-tagged: breakdown, planned maintenance, material shortage, changeover, operator absence. Pareto analysis built in.

Production vs. Plan Tracking

Compare actual output against shift target at every moment. Visual traffic-light indicators flag underperforming lines instantly.

Bottleneck Identification

Automatically identifies the slowest station in each production line that is limiting overall throughput. Prioritise interventions smartly.

Operator-Level Accountability

Assign operators to machines per shift. Compare operator-wise output, speed, and rejection rate. Identify training needs objectively.

Digital Andon & Escalation

Auto-Andon when a machine stops. Escalation timers alert supervisors, maintenance teams, and managers if issues are not resolved within SLA time.

Energy Per Unit Integration

When combined with EMS, view energy cost per unit produced per machine. Identify the most energy-wasteful processes instantly.

How It Works

1
Connect — Hardware sensors or protocol adapters are installed on your machines (typically in 1–3 days per line). No machine modification needed.
2
Stream — Every machine cycle, production count, and stop event is streamed in real time to the Petrus Edge Device and then to the cloud/on-premise server.
3
Classify — The AI engine automatically classifies stoppages, computes OEE components, and compares actuals vs. plan targets without human intervention.
4
Visualise — Live dashboards, shift reports, trend graphs, and Pareto charts are available on any browser — TV screens on the floor, tablets for supervisors, or desktops for managers.
5
Alert — When something goes wrong, the right person is notified immediately — via app, SMS, or WhatsApp — with full context and escalation if unresolved.
