What is the Assembly Tracking System?

The Petrus ATS digitises your entire assembly process — from work instructions to torque verification and Poka-Yoke gates that block operators from advancing if a step is incomplete or out of spec.

Every unit is assigned a unique ID at line start. As it passes each station, operator, measurements, confirmations, and deviations are captured — creating a full assembly history per serial number.

Andon lights, escalation timers, and WIP dashboards give supervisors real-time visibility of all stations simultaneously from any device.

ATS Capabilities:

  • ✓ Digital work instructions per station
  • ✓ Step-by-step operator guidance with photos
  • ✓ Poka-Yoke mandatory step confirmation
  • ✓ Torque tool & PLC integration
  • ✓ Barcode / QR scanning at each station
  • ✓ Andon escalation with configurable timers
  • ✓ Real-time WIP dashboard across all stations
  • ✓ Full unit-level assembly genealogy

Core Features

Digital SOPs per Station

Rich work instructions with images and videos — mounted on screens at each station. Version-controlled and change-logged automatically.

Poka-Yoke Step Validation

Each step must be positively confirmed before the operator proceeds. Wrong component scan or out-of-spec torque blocks forward movement.

Real-Time WIP Tracking

See every unit at every station simultaneously. Identify accumulation queues and idle stations without walking the assembly line.

Digital Andon System

Operator help calls trigger Andon lights and escalation timers. Supervisors are notified automatically if unresolved within SLA.

Torque & Tool Integration

Auto-reading from smart wrenches or PLC tools. Torque values verified against spec before the next step is unlocked.

Station Cycle-Time Analytics

Compare actual vs. standard cycle time per station. Detect which steps cause delay and focus improvement effort precisely.
